Ace App Information

The Ace Airlines App is designed to provide the customer with an easy to use way of booking flights, checking in, viewing flights, and printing boarding passes. It started off with Ace App Alpha. This version included only the bare minimum. You could book a flight and check-in. Now with Ace App BETA, you have many more features. It will allow you to Book a Flight and then save it to your account. You will be able to check in to your flights from your phone. You will be able to check your flight status in real-time before you even get to the airport. You will be able to view all of your flights on a single page. The Ace Airlines App will bring allow for Ace Airlines to further interact with its customers. It will also provide a platform on which Ace Airlines can further develop. If you are part of the Ace Airlines Newsletter, you know about the upcoming electronic boarding passes. Starting in the Summer of 2020 Ace Airlines will be providing the option of checking in to your flight and printing your boarding pass via the Ace Airlines App.

What is FlightDude?

This question is asked by many people. FlightDude is not a company. FlightDude is a personal website created by a middle schooler in the United States. FlightDude has created many incredible development projects which are showcased right here on flightdude.net. There are two main divisions of FlightDude. One being the projects, developed using front-end HTML, CSS, and JS, and Node.js as Server-side. Some of FlightDude's projects were developed for personal purposes, and some for educational purposes. We at FlightDude hope you enjoy these projects, whether it'd be for personal or educational use. Thank you for your interest in FlightDude.
